Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026. Last Updated: 2026. This Privacy Policy explains how Vellow collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information. It is incorporated by reference into our Terms & Conditions.

1. Introduction and Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how the operator of the Vellow online storefront ("Vellow," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information when you visit our website (the "Site") and place orders. Vellow is a United States–based storefront. By using the Site or placing an order, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy.

We offer guest checkout only. We do not create password-protected customer accounts, and we do not ask you to create or maintain login credentials with us.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Identifiers and contact information: your name, email address, and shipping address.
  • Order information: the items you order, order value, and related transaction details.
  • Communications: information you provide when you contact us for support or to open a dispute.

Payment information (processed by our payment processor, not stored by us): Card payments are processed by Stripe. Your full payment card number and related sensitive card data are collected and processed directly by Stripe and are not stored by Vellow. We may receive limited transaction confirmation data from Stripe (for example, that a payment succeeded, a partial card identifier, or a payment reference) to manage your order.

Information collected automatically: your IP address, device and browser type, and similar technical identifiers, together with information about how you interact with the Site, collected through cookies and similar technologies (see Section 6).

We do not intentionally collect Social Security numbers, government ID numbers, precise geolocation, biometric data, or special categories of sensitive personal information, and we ask that you not provide such information to us.

3. How and Why We Use Your Information

We use personal information to:

  • Process, verify, authorize, capture payment for, and fulfill your orders;
  • Transmit your order and shipping details to our third-party fulfillment suppliers and carriers so your order can be shipped;
  • Communicate with you about your order, including confirmations, shipping and tracking updates, and support or dispute responses;
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, unauthorized transactions, chargeback abuse, security incidents, and other misuse;
  • Operate, maintain, secure, and improve the Site;
  • Comply with our legal, tax, accounting, and regulatory obligations and to establish, exercise, or defend legal claims.

4. Legal Bases for Processing (Where Applicable)

Where a legal-basis framework applies to you, we rely on: performance of a contract (to process and fulfill your orders and provide support); legitimate interests (to secure the Site, prevent fraud and abuse, and improve our services, balanced against your rights); legal obligation (to meet tax, accounting, consumer-protection, and record-keeping requirements); and consent (where required, for certain cookies or communications, which you may withdraw at any time where processing is based on consent).

5. How We Share Your Information; Third Parties and Processors

We do not sell your personal information (see Section 9). We share personal information only as follows:

  • Payment processing: Stripe, which processes your payment under its own privacy policy.
  • Fulfillment and shipping: third-party suppliers, warehouses, and shipping carriers who receive the information necessary to package, ship, and deliver your order (such as your name, shipping address, and order details), and to handle returns or disputes.
  • Technology providers: our website hosting provider and, where used, analytics and security service providers who process technical and usage data on our behalf.
  • Legal and safety: government authorities, courts, or other parties where we believe disclosure is required by law or legal process, or is necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others, or to enforce our Terms.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, subject to this Policy.

6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the Site (for example, to maintain your cart and secure the checkout session), to remember preferences, and, where used, to understand Site usage through analytics. Some cookies are strictly necessary for the Site to function. You can control non-essential cookies through your browser settings; disabling certain cookies may affect Site functionality. Where required by law, we will request your consent for non-essential cookies.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, including to complete your order, provide support, resolve disputes and chargebacks, and comply with our legal, tax, and accounting obligations, after which we delete or de-identify it. Retention periods vary by data type and by applicable legal requirements.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. Card data is handled by Stripe, a PCI-DSS-compliant payment processor, and is not stored on our systems. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot and do not guarantee absolute security. You provide information to us at your own risk.

9. Sale and Sharing of Personal Information; "Do Not Sell or Share"

Vellow does not sell your personal information, and we do not share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising, in each case as those terms are defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (the "CCPA"). Because we do not sell or share personal information, no "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" opt-out action is required to stop such activity; nonetheless, you may contact us at any time with questions or requests using the details in Section 16.

10. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have some or all of the following rights, subject to legal limits and verification: the right to know or access the personal information we hold about you; the right to request correction of inaccurate personal information; the right to request deletion of your personal information; the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (note: we do not sell or share personal information); the right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights; and, where applicable, the right to data portability, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to withdraw consent.

Your California Rights (CCPA/CPRA): If you are a California resident, you have the rights described above, including the right to know, delete, and correct, and the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information as defined by the CCPA. You may exercise your rights as described in Section 12, and you may use an authorized agent to submit a request on your behalf, subject to our verification of the agent's authority and your identity. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

Other U.S. State Rights: Residents of other U.S. states with comprehensive privacy laws may have similar rights to access, correct, delete, and opt out of certain processing. We honor applicable rights consistent with the law of your state. If we deny a request, you may have a right to appeal by contacting us using the details in Section 16.

11. Children's Privacy

The Site is intended for adults and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16, and no one under 13 may use the Site or provide personal information. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

12. How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of the rights described above, contact us by email at vellowretail@gmail.com with the subject line "Privacy Request" and a description of your request. To protect your privacy and security, we will take steps to verify your identity before responding, typically by matching information you provide (such as your order email and details) with our records. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law. There is generally no charge to make a request, though we may charge a reasonable fee or decline requests that are excessive, repetitive, or manifestly unfounded, as permitted by law.

13. International Users; U.S.-Only Operation

The Site is operated from the United States and is intended for customers in the United States. If you access the Site from outside the United States, you understand that your information will be processed in the United States, where privacy laws may differ from those of your location. We do not represent that the Site or our practices comply with the laws of any jurisdiction outside the United States.

14. Third-Party Links and Services

The Site may reference or link to third-party websites or services (including Stripe and carrier tracking pages) that we do not control. This Policy does not apply to those third parties, and we are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third party you interact with.

15. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date above and, where required by law, provide additional notice. Changes are effective when posted unless a later effective date is stated. Your continued use of the Site after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy to the extent permitted by law.

16. Contact Us

If you have questions or requests regarding this Policy or your personal information, contact: Vellow, Attn: Privacy, vellowretail@gmail.com.
